    Common Questions

    Frequently Asked

    What age group do you accept?
    We work with children aged 2 to 18 years.
    Which disabilities are accepted for admission?
    We work with children with Cerebral Palsy, ASD, Learning Disability, Down's Syndrome, and Slow Learners.
    What is the admission process?
    A basic assessment to understand the level of the child, followed by a discussion with parents. There is no assessment fee.
    What are the school timings?
    We operate six days a week. School hours are 9:30 AM to 2:00 PM.
    What therapies do you provide?
    We provide Physiotherapy, Occupational Therapy, Speech Therapy, Special Education, and Remedial services.
